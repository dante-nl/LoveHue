LoveHue

Animations for everyone.

Animations can be used to enhance the experience with your lights.

We use .lhad files to show them, they can easily be created and modified.

Not just colors.

Animations can also change your brightness very easily.

It's as simple as adding "brightness": 50 to set your brightness to 50%.

Basic features included.

Turn the lights on and off and more

LoveHue also supports "features" such as turning on/off lights and setting the brightness manually.
